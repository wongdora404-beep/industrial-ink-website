Application-Based Inkjet Solutions

Practical inkjet printing solutions designed around real production requirements and operating conditions.

Our Approach

Instead of promoting a single product for all scenarios, we focus on understanding application conditions and recommending suitable inkjet solutions that perform reliably in daily production.

  • Substrate material evaluation
  • Production speed and environment analysis
  • Compatibility with existing inkjet systems
  • Long-term stability considerations

This approach helps reduce unexpected downtime and improves consistency across different production batches.

Typical Applications

Our inkjet solutions are commonly used in the following industrial identification and marking applications.

Packaging Coding

Date codes, batch numbers, and traceability information on cartons, films, and flexible packaging materials.

Product Identification

Logos, text, and variable data printing on industrial products and components.

Label & Variable Data Printing

On-demand variable data printing for labels and short production runs.
