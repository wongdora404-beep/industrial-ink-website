WB-220 Water-Based Inkjet Ink

Stable, reliable ink for industrial inkjet systems with consistent jetting and long production runs.

Product Overview

WB-220 is engineered for industrial applications where long-term stability, consistent jetting performance, and production reliability are critical.

It is suitable for a wide range of industrial printing systems and substrates, helping reduce downtime and maintain stable output quality.

Key Benefits

  • Stable viscosity and surface tension for consistent jetting
  • Reduced nozzle clogging during continuous operation
  • Good adhesion on common industrial substrates
  • Compatible with major industrial inkjet printheads
  • Reliable batch-to-batch consistency

Typical Applications

  • Packaging and labeling
  • Industrial coding and marking
  • Decorative and functional printing
  • Customized industrial graphics
