WB-220 Water-Based Inkjet Ink

Stable, reliable water-based ink designed for industrial inkjet systems requiring consistent jetting and long production runs.

Request Technical Details

Key Benefits

  • Stable viscosity and surface tension for consistent jetting
  • Reduced nozzle clogging during continuous operation
  • Good adhesion on a wide range of substrates
  • Designed for industrial production environments

Typical Applications

WB-220 is suitable for industrial inkjet printing applications where stability, consistency and reliability are critical.

  • Decorative surface printing
  • Building materials printing
  • Packaging and labeling
  • Customized industrial printing

Compatibility

WB-220 is developed to be compatible with major industrial inkjet printheads and systems. Specific compatibility and performance depend on system configuration and operating conditions.

We recommend application testing before mass production to ensure optimal results.
