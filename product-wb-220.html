WB-220 Water-based Ink

Stable, high-density water-based ink for highspeed signage printing.

Overview

WB-220 is a high-performance water-based ink designed for continuous industrial printing. Excellent fluidity minimizes printhead clogging and delivers consistent color across large print runs.

Technical Specifications

  • Viscosity: 3–7 cP
  • Color: CMYK (pigment/dye options)
  • Shelf life: 12 months (5–25°C)
  • Compatibility: Epson DX5/DX7, Ricoh GEN5, Konica KM512
